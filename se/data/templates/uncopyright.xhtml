This is an Uncopyright page. Where a copyright page tells you what you cannot do, this page exists to tell you what you can: everything. The literary works of Leo Tolstoy reproduced here were first published in the 19th and early 20th centuries, and their copyright has long expired in all jurisdictions.

Copyright laws are different all over the world. While the source texts are in the public domain, if you are uncertain about the copyright status of any material in your jurisdiction, you should check your local laws before using this ebook.

Non-authorship activities performed on items that are in the public domain⁠—so-called “sweat of the brow" work⁠—don’t create a new copyright. That means that nobody can claim a new copyright on an item that is in the public domain for, among other things, work like digitization, markup, or typography.
